# Admin Guide: Teams & Invites

## Overview

Teams and Invites let you control who can see and join specific exercises.

**Three visibility options:**
- **Public** - Anyone can see and join
- **Team Only** - Only members of assigned teams can see
- **Invite Only** - Only individually invited users can see

---

## Managing Teams

### Access Teams Page
1. Go to **Admin Dashboard**
2. Click **Manage Teams**

### Create a Team
1. Click **New Team**
2. Enter team name (e.g., "PhD Students Cohort 1")
3. Add optional description
4. Click **Create Team**

### Add Members to a Team
1. Click the **Users icon** (ðŸ‘¥) on the team card
2. Search for users by email or name
3. Click a user to add them
4. Use **Prev/Next** to browse all users

### Remove Members
1. Open the team members dialog
2. Click the **X** next to the member's name

---

## Creating Team-Only Exercises

1. Go to **Admin > Exercises**
2. Click **New Exercise** or edit existing
3. Set **Visibility** to **Team Only**
4. Click team badges to assign teams (selected = filled badge)
5. Save the exercise

**Result:** Only users in the selected teams will see this exercise in their "My Teams" tab.

---

## Creating Invite-Only Exercises

1. Create/edit an exercise
2. Set **Visibility** to **Invite Only**
3. Save the exercise
4. In the exercise list, click the **UserPlus icon** (ðŸ‘¤+)
5. Search and click users to invite them

### Managing Invites
- **Pending** - User hasn't joined yet
- **Accepted** - User has joined the exercise
- Click **X** to remove an invite

---

## What Participants See

### Exercises Page Tabs
- **Public** - All public exercises
- **My Teams** - Exercises assigned to their teams
- **My Invites** - Exercises they've been invited to

### Notification Badge
- Red badge on "Exercises" in header shows pending invite count
- Disappears when no pending invites

---

## Best Practices

### When to Use Teams
- Recurring groups (classes, departments, research cohorts)
- Multiple exercises for the same group
- Easier than inviting individuals repeatedly

### When to Use Invites
- One-off participants
- Mixed groups from different teams
- External collaborators

### Combining Approaches
You can have:
- Some public exercises for general participation
- Team exercises for specific cohorts
- Invite-only exercises for special cases

---

## FAQ

**Q: Can a user be in multiple teams?**
A: Yes, they'll see exercises from all their teams.

**Q: What happens if I remove someone from a team mid-exercise?**
A: They lose access to team-only exercises but keep their existing participation data.

**Q: Can I change visibility after participants have joined?**
A: Yes, but be careful - changing to team_only or invite_only may hide the exercise from current participants who aren't in the team/invited.

**Q: Do team members auto-join exercises?**
A: No, team membership gives visibility. Users still need to click "Join" to participate.
